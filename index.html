About

Welcome to My Creative Journey!

Hey there! I'm Alberto, passionate graphic designer with a penchant for bringing ideas to life through visuals. Originally from Portugal, I've been calling Italy home since the age of 4. After graduating from the renowned Mirano, Levi-Ponti high school in Italy, I delved into the captivating world of Computer Science.

It was during my explorations that stumbled upon the enchanting realm of Photoshop. As if struck by artistic lightning, I realized my true calling lay in graphic design. From that moment on, I embarked on a thrilling ride, crafting graphics that not only amazed my friends but also filled me with immense joy and purpose.

My design journey has been a quest for continuous improvement and creative expression. Through my portfolio, I aim to showcase the fusion of my diverse influences and experiences, culminating in striking visuals that leave an indelible mark.
